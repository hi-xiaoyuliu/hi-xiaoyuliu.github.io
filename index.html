About Me

I obtained the Ph.D. degree from the Delft Center for Systems and Control, Delft University of Technology, Delft, the Netherlands, in 2024, the M.Sc. and B.Sc. degrees from Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU), Beijing, China, in 2020 and 2017, respectively. In 2024, I was a Visiting Scholar with the Division of Decision and Control Systems, KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden.

Research Interests

  • Model predictive control

  • Distributed Control

  • Control of hybrid systems

  • Reinforcement learning

  • Railway operations

  • Intelligent vehicles
