About Me

I received the B.Sc. and M.Sc. degree from Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU), Beijing, China, in 2017 and 2020, respectively. Currently, I am working towards the Ph.D. degree in the Delft Center for Systems and Control, Delft University of Technology, Delft, the Netherlands, under the supervision of Prof. Bart De Schutter and Prof. Azita Dabiri.

Research Interests

  • Model predictive control

  • Control of hybrid systems

  • Railway operations

  • Reinforcement learning

  • Intelligent transportation systems
